# Business Module

Vanilla business scope:

- customers
- products and services
- invoices
- double-entry ledger and SKR03-style chart of accounts
- inbound receipts with OCR/review state
- payment and bank reconciliation status
- DATEV-style bookkeeping exports
- finance reports

This module starts deliberately small, but the default surface is real
bookkeeping: accounts, journal entries, receipts, bank matches, exports, and
reports are functional starter workflows. Country-specific depth beyond the
included German-friendly defaults should be added as customer customization,
not as CTOX core behavior.
